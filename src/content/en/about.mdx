# About

**IntD** is a small tool that demonstrates an **Int**eger **D**ecomposition algorithm.

Enter a target total, set each item's price range and last-digit rules, and it enumerates every combination, then lists the top three plans ranked by your chosen preference (fewest plastic bags, or balanced item quantities).

When you are curious how a given total could be split into several items (such as a bento or a drink) and a one-dollar item (such as a plastic bag), **IntD** helps you list a few reasonable, natural combinations.

For the full solving logic and complexity analysis, see the [algorithm notes](/en/algo).
